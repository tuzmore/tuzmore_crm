🚀 About Tuzmore

Tuzmore is a modern CRM SaaS designed to simplify sales, boost productivity, and empower businesses with tools that truly make a difference.

Why Tuzmore Was Born 🚀

We saw businesses struggling every day — losing time, money, and clients. Emails were scattered,
payments were delayed, meetings were missed, and
valuable leads slipped away. Work wasn’t organized, and
teams wasted hours managing tools instead of growing their business.

That’s why Tuzmore was created: to bring everything into one smart, easy-to-use dashboard.
With Tuzmore, no more chaos — you manage your clients, leads, projects, payments, and meetings all in one place,
saving time so you can focus on what really matters: growing your business.

🚀 Our Mission

To empower small and growing businesses with enterprise-grade CRM tools that are easy to use, affordable, and scalable. Every team — from startups to enterprises — deserves access to powerful software that drives growth.

🌍 Our Vision

We envision a world where businesses spend less time fighting messy spreadsheets and more time closing deals, delighting customers, and scaling up. Tuzmore makes CRM simple, modern, and accessible to everyone.

Lost Opportunities

Never miss a lead again. Track pipelines, measure revenue, and close deals with automated insights.
